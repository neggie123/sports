Sports blogs are a type of blog that allows fans of a given sport to share sporting news and opinions on sports-related topics. Sports blogs can cover any number of sports, including football, basketball, soccer, UFC, NASCAR, golf, and hockey. Blogging has been around since the 1990s and could help lead to a career in sports journalism or game analysis.
